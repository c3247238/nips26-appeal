# Planning Critique

## Planning success

The planning correction was strategically sound. The project stopped reopening method-expansion branches and instead packaged the strongest defensible claim from the available evidence.

## Remaining planning gap

The current plan still under-specifies the last mile from evidence bundle to submission-ready artifact. In particular, reviewer-facing reproducibility, related-work sharpening, and camera-ready polish are treated as small cleanup items even though they matter disproportionately for a narrow negative-case paper.

## Recommendation

The next planning pass should be a packaging-and-submission checklist rather than a scientific redesign. Prioritize claim framing, artifact-release language, related-work precision, and final LaTeX polish.
