# Ideation Critique

## What improved

The project no longer tries to force a positive controller paper out of ambiguous evidence. That is the right ideation correction, and it is the main reason the current paper is credible.

## Remaining ideation concern

The current framing still sits between two identities: a negative-case study and an audit-template paper. That hybrid can work, but only if the paper never implies that one audited case is enough to establish a broadly transferable protocol contribution.

## Best next step

Treat the idea contribution as narrowly interpretive: a reviewer-safe demonstration that sham controls can overturn a plausible small-gain DLM story. Anything broader should be pushed into future work, not kept half-alive in the main contribution language.
