# Final Critic Review

## Overall Assessment

This manuscript is now much better aligned with its evidence than the earlier method-forward draft. The paper makes the right strategic move: it treats the contribution as a compute-normalized diagnostic/protocol study rather than as a new controller paper. That framing is consistent with the current assets and gives the work a clearer, more defensible identity.

The remaining issue is no longer conceptual drift. It is finish quality. The paper now has a coherent thesis, a section-complete draft, a bucket-level mechanism layer, a runtime-lineage bundle, and a minimal seed closure. What still keeps it below a comfortable top-tier submission threshold is the narrow empirical footprint, incomplete citation scaffolding, and the fact that some protocol/figure assets are still represented more as text contracts than fully polished submission artifacts.

## 1. Novelty and Significance

### Strengths

- The most novel contribution is the **evaluation framing**: bucket-level outcomes plus runtime-lineage auditability for training-free DLM revision.
- The paper is honest about what it is not claiming, which materially increases credibility.
- Closing the optional controller probe as `NO_GO` is a strength, not a weakness; it shows disciplined scope control.

### Weaknesses

- The paper is still better described as a strong diagnostic correction than as a major new algorithmic advance.
- Because the controller contribution is intentionally downscoped, the paper's significance depends heavily on methodological rigor and presentation quality.

## 2. Technical Soundness

### Strengths

- The argument now matches the actual evidence.
- The bucket audit gives the GSM8K gain a real mechanism-facing explanation: `7 fixed`, `4 harmed`, `89 no_effect`.
- The runtime probe and protocol bundle make the comparison auditable rather than implicit.
- The seed spot-check is correctly written as **sign consistency**, not robustness.

### Weaknesses

- The main evidence still centers on a single headline pair and a focused audited slice.
- The protocol perspective is clear, but the final submission will still need rendered figures/tables and explicit citation support to feel fully closed.

## 3. Clarity and Presentation

### Strengths

- Section ordering is strong and coherent.
- The integrated draft stays consistent with the new diagnostic/protocol positioning.
- Negative and limiting evidence is reported responsibly rather than hidden.

### Weaknesses

- Related Work remains too light for a final conference submission.
- Some sections still read like a strong advanced draft rather than a fully matured camera-ready manuscript.
- The paper will benefit from a tighter abstract/introduction pass to reduce duplicated phrasing.

## 4. Experimental Rigor

### Strengths

1. The paper no longer depends on aggregate-only evidence.
2. The headline pair now has a minimal uncertainty check.
3. Runtime assumptions are explicit rather than buried in prose.

### Remaining concerns

1. The seed check is deliberately minimal and cannot substitute for a broader robustness study.
2. The main bucket evidence is still centered on one audited slice.
3. Cross-task evidence should remain strictly boundary/positioning evidence.

## 5. Reproducibility

### Strengths

- Claim-to-asset lineage is now materially better than before.
- The integrated draft clearly points to the main JSON assets behind its claims.
- The runtime contract is explicit enough that another reader could understand the execution envelope.

### Weaknesses

- The final submission package still needs rendered visual assets and tighter appendix presentation.
- Citation scaffolding and manuscript polish are not yet at final-submission standard.

## Acceptance Outlook

This is now a credible diagnostic/protocol paper in spirit. It is stronger than a workshop-style exploratory draft and closer to a submission-ready manuscript than the earlier iterations. However, I would still treat it as a **serious revision away** from a confident top-tier submission because the empirical scope remains narrow and the paper package is not fully polished.

## Highest-Value Revisions

1. Add citation anchors and strengthen Related Work.
2. Render the remaining protocol/runtime figures and tables.
3. Tighten the abstract/introduction wording so the contribution is stated once, cleanly.
4. Present the runtime-lineage appendix in a more reviewer-friendly final format.

## Final Verdict

This is now the right paper and a substantially stronger one. The evidence base is still narrower than ideal, but the manuscript finally uses that evidence honestly. With one more editorial/presentation pass, it can become a credible submission in the diagnostic-study lane.

SCORE: 7.2
