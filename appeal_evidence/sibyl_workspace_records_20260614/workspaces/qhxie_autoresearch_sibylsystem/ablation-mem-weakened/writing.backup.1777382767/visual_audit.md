## Issues Found and Fixed in This Revision

1. **H3 reporting was confusing** (Major): The original Table 1 had a "See below" placeholder for H3. Fixed by adding a dedicated Table 1a with clear columns for slope signs, CV, and verdict per hypothesis.

2. **Figure 1 PDF was reported missing** (Minor): The review noted only a TikZ description existed. In fact, `fig1_pipeline.pdf` was already generated by `gen_fig1_pipeline.py`. Verified the PDF exists (34KB).

3. **Table 2 caption accuracy** (Minor): The caption says "Top 8 most absorbed features at layers 4 and 8" and the data correctly shows only layers 4 and 8. No layer 10 features are present. Caption is accurate.

## Suggestions for Additional Visuals

The paper is well-balanced between text and visuals. No additional figures are strictly necessary. Optional enhancements for a future revision:
